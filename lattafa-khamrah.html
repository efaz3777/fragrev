Overview

Lattafa Khamrah has become one of the most talked-about fragrances in recent years. Released by Lattafa Perfumes, Khamrah delivers a rich, luxurious gourmand experience packed with cinnamon, dates, praline, vanilla, amber, and woods. Despite its affordable price, it offers performance and quality that rival fragrances many times its cost.

Often praised for its warm, cozy, and addictive scent profile, Khamrah has developed a cult following among fragrance enthusiasts worldwide. It is a fragrance designed for cooler weather and special occasions where its sweet and spicy character can truly shine.

Detailed Review

Khamrah opens with a delicious burst of cinnamon and warm spices that immediately create a cozy atmosphere. The opening feels rich and luxurious, almost like stepping into a bakery filled with fresh pastries and sweet desserts.

As the fragrance develops, sweet dates and praline take center stage. This stage is where Khamrah becomes truly addictive. The sweetness is dense and decadent without becoming overly synthetic.

Several hours later, vanilla, amberwood and benzoin create a smooth, creamy dry-down that can remain detectable for well over ten hours. On clothing, Khamrah often lasts until the next day.

While not the most versatile fragrance for hot summer weather, Khamrah excels during autumn and winter. It is perfect for evenings, date nights, social gatherings and festive occasions.

Who Should Buy Khamrah?

Khamrah is ideal for anyone who enjoys sweet fragrances with a rich gourmand character. If you love notes like cinnamon, vanilla, caramel, dates, amber and praline, this fragrance deserves a place in your collection.

Beginners will appreciate its affordability, while experienced collectors often keep it because of its incredible performance and crowd-pleasing scent profile.
